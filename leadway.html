Undergraduate Admissions


As an institution at the forefront of innovation and technology and in view of the persisting Covid-19 pandemic, propective candidates can begin to process their admission into Leadway University, for the 2021/2022 academic year, online, without traveling to the campus. The procedure is simple:



  1. Download the admission from here, complete it and gather all requisite documents.
  2. Pay the appilcation fee (a non-refundable sum of N10,000 only), to Leadway University, Zenith Bank Account number: 1012380571.
  3. Send a scanned image of the admission form to admissions@lwu.edu.ng


General Requirements

The minimum entry requirement for admission into Leadway University is GCE O/L, SSCE/NECO or equivalent credit level pass in five(5) subjects, including English Language, and Mathematics.
